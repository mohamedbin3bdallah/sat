Systems and Technology (SAT) is a supplier and manufacturer of pumps, filters, among other engineering equipment to serve a wide range of industries all over Egypt, North Africa, and the Middle East. For 25 years, SAT has been the sole distributor for Serfilco Ltd., a manufacturer and supplier of quality equipment for fluid handling and surface finishing applications and industries with over 40 years of experience.
